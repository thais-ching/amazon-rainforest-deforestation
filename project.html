Learn More about this Project

My name is Thais Ching and I am originally from Lima, Peru. Ever since I was young, I have had a huge admiration for the Amazon Rainforest. I wanted to undertake this project to raise awareness about the critical situation facing the Amazon Rainforest. Once considered the world’s largest carbon sink, parts of the forest now emit more carbon dioxide than they absorb, largely due to deforestation. This has not only put the rainforest's biodiversity at risk but also disrupted regional weather patterns, contributing to droughts and increasing the frequency of wildfires. Understanding these environmental impacts and their devastating cycle of deforestation is essential in highlighting the urgency of protecting the Amazon.
